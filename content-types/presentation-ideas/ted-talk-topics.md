# TED-Style Talk Topic Collection

This document provides curated TED-style presentation topics suitable for education, leadership, and personal development talks.

---

## What Defines a Strong Topic

| Criterion | Description |
|--------|------------|
| Relevance | Matters to audience |
| Clarity | Easy to explain |
| Insight | Offers a new angle |
| Impact | Inspires reflection |

---

## Topic Examples

### Students
- Redefining success beyond grades
- Learning from failure
- Curiosity as a career skill

### Educators
- Teaching beyond curriculum
- Encouraging independent thinking
- Building resilience in learning

### Professionals
- Psychological safety at work
- Decision-making under uncertainty
- Ethics in leadership

---

## Presentation Tip

Strong talks focus on **one core idea**, supported by stories and evidence.
