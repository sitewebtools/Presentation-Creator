## Key Highlights from the Original Article

### 1. Mentimeter
- **Best for:** Live polls and audience feedback.
- **Pros:** Simple and intuitive, wide range of interactive features, real‑time analytics.
- **Cons:** Limited design customisation, free plan restricts interactive questions.
- **Pricing:** Free plan available; paid plans from $13/month.

### 2. AiPPT
- **Best for:** Fast, data‑driven, well‑designed interactive presentations.
- **Pros:** User‑friendly dashboard, built‑in professional charts, link sharing for collaboration, export to PDF/PPTX/images, no watermark.
- **Cons:** Requires internet connection for AI functions; free plan has limitations.
- **Pricing:** Free plan; Premium from $5/month (yearly subscription).

### 3. Slido
- **Best for:** Corporate meetings and webinars.
- **Pros:** Real‑time moderation, pre‑built quiz templates, ranking and competition features.
- **Cons:** Limited slide design capabilities; advanced features require paid plans.
- **Pricing:** Free plan; Engage from $17.50/month.

### 4. AhaSlides
- **Best for:** Classrooms and workshops.
- **Pros:** Gamified quizzes and polls, easy to use, supports live Q&A, word clouds, audience ranking.
- **Cons:** Limited design templates; advanced features behind paywall.
- **Pricing:** Free plan; Essential from $7.95/month.

### 5. Prezi
- **Best for:** Storytelling and business pitches.
- **Pros:** Unique zooming canvas, supports multimedia, collaboration tools, analytics.
- **Cons:** Learning curve; limited traditional slide layouts.
- **Pricing:** Free plan; Standard from $7/month.

### 6. Canva Presentations
- **Best for:** Students and startups.
- **Pros:** Full creative control, supports animated slides, embedded videos, clickable links, huge template library.
- **Cons:** Limited advanced data visualisation; premium elements require paid plan.
- **Pricing:** Free plan; Pro from $15/month.

### 7. Kahoot!
- **Best for:** Education and training.
- **Pros:** Gamified quizzes, easy participation via device codes, real‑time scoring and leaderboards.
- **Cons:** Limited design flexibility; advanced features require paid plans.
- **Pricing:** Free plan; Kahoot!+ Bronze from $3.99/month.

### 8. Visme
- **Best for:** Marketers and educators.
- **Pros:** Drag‑and‑drop editor, animated graphics, multimedia integration, real‑time collaboration, analytics.
- **Cons:** Limited free templates.
- **Pricing:** Free plan; Starter from $12.25/month.

### 9. Zoho Show
- **Best for:** Businesses using the Zoho suite.
- **Pros:** Real‑time collaboration with version control, supports polls, Q&A, clickable links, cloud access.
- **Cons:** Limited advanced animation options.
- **Pricing:** Free for individuals; Professional from $2.5/user/month.

### 10. Beautiful.ai
- **Best for:** Professionals needing quick design.
- **Pros:** AI‑powered slide design, supports interactive charts and clickable links, cloud‑based access.
- **Cons:** Limited free templates.
- **Pricing:** Pro from $12/month.

### 11. Pitch
- **Best for:** Startups and team pitches.
- **Pros:** Real‑time collaboration with version control, supports interactive elements, integrates with productivity tools, analytics.
- **Cons:** Limited free templates.
- **Pricing:** Free plan; Pro from $20/month.

### 12. Genially
- **Best for:** Creative storytelling and e‑learning.
- **Pros:** Wide range of interactive elements, large template library, multimedia integration, gamification, real‑time collaboration.
- **Cons:** Can be overwhelming for beginners; paid plan required for branding and advanced interactivity.
- **Pricing:** Free plan; Pro from $7.49/month.
