## Key Takeaways from the Article

### What Actually Matters When AI Builds Your Deck

After testing every major AI presentation platform for three months, the author found that tools delivering usable results shared three traits:

1. **They ask detailed questions upfront** – audience type, presentation goal, desired tone.
2. **They handle messy input gracefully** – documents, URLs, notes.
3. **They understand context** – a sales pitch requires different output than an academic conference.

Most tools can generate slides fast. The real question is how much time you’ll spend fixing what they created.

### The Speed‑Quality Balance Nobody Talks About

The author tested systematically: a 20‑slide product launch presentation on five platforms.

- **Generation times:** 75 seconds to 3 minutes.
- **Revision times:** 15 minutes to 2 hours.

Tools requiring minimal cleanup shared one pattern: **they gathered detailed context upfront**. Tools relying solely on topic keywords consistently produced generic output requiring extensive rework.

AiPPT’s multi‑model approach addresses this directly – lighter models deliver in 90 seconds for quick internal drafts, while advanced models add minimal generation time but cut revision work by 70%.

### Language Support That Actually Works

English‑first tools consistently fail with non‑Latin scripts – broken layouts, illegible text, spacing disasters.

AiPPT supports **43 languages** with proper font selection and layout adaptation for each. This is not just translation – it’s culturally appropriate design conventions.

### Visual Quality: Where Most Tools Still Fail

AI‑generated visuals have improved, but quality varies wildly. Some platforms still rely on generic stock photos that scream “template.”

**Nano Banana Mode** (AiPPT) grounds visuals in real‑world scenes through Google Real‑Time Search – actual solar installations and wind farms, not abstract “green energy” clip art. The built‑in AI image generator (Nano Banana Pro) creates custom visuals with native 4K support.
