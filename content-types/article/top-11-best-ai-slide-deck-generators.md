## Key Highlights from the Original Article

### 1. Gamma – AI Storytelling Slides
- **Pros:** Narrative‑driven organisation, interactive elements, real‑time collaboration
- **Cons:** Limited offline editing, fewer template variations

### 2. AiPPT – Instant Slide Decks from Documents
- **Pros:** Chat‑based outline generation, instant creation from text/documents, 200k+ templates, no watermark
- **Cons:** Some advanced layouts need fine‑tuning

### 3. Beautiful.ai – Smart, Polished Templates
- **Pros:** Auto‑adjusting templates, professional design, beginner‑friendly
- **Cons:** Limited advanced customisation, few animation options

### 4. Canva – Flexible AI Slide Design
- **Pros:** Highly flexible, AI‑assisted generation, vast template library, collaboration‑friendly
- **Cons:** Slightly slower for large decks, can be overwhelming for beginners

### 5. GenPPT – Fast Deck Creation
- **Pros:** Extremely fast, logical content organisation, consistent formatting
- **Cons:** Limited customisation, fewer template options

### 6. Visme – Data‑Rich Presentations
- **Pros:** AI‑assisted creation, rich charts and infographics, interactive elements
- **Cons:** Steeper learning curve, premium required for advanced features

### 7. Tome – Narrative Multimedia Slides
- **Pros:** Story‑driven organisation, multimedia integration, AI‑assisted suggestions
- **Cons:** Limited offline functionality, fewer template customisations

### 8. Slidesgo – Editable Free Templates
- **Pros:** Wide range of free editable templates, easy customisation, beginner‑friendly
- **Cons:** Limited automatic slide generation, requires manual editing

### 9. Simplified – Quick Branded Slides
- **Pros:** Fast creation with brand consistency, AI‑powered suggestions, industry‑specific templates
- **Cons:** Limited design flexibility, premium required for advanced features

### 10. Kroma.ai – Professional Data Decks
- **Pros:** Professional data‑rich slides, customisable business templates, efficient polished output
- **Cons:** Learning curve for beginners, premium for full features

### 11. HubSpot Clip Creator – Animated Marketing Slides
- **Pros:** Multimedia integration, marketing‑focused templates, engaging dynamic presentations
- **Cons:** Limited flexibility for non‑marketing topics, premium for advanced features
