## What Is a PowerPoint Night?

A PowerPoint Night is a social activity where friends create short, funny presentations on random topics and share them. The goal is pure entertainment – spark creativity, share inside jokes, and enjoy lots of laughs together. This trend has exploded on social media because it transforms traditional presentations into fun, memorable evenings.

---

## How to Host a Successful PowerPoint Night

- **Choose a comfortable setting** – lounges, dorms, or living rooms work best.
- **Set clear rules** – limit slides to 5–8 per person and cap presentations at 5 minutes.
- **Be creative** – use funny images, infographics, GIFs, memes, and dramatic transitions.
- **Engage the audience** – make it interactive with games or voting (e.g., “Funniest Slide”, “Most Creative Topic”).
