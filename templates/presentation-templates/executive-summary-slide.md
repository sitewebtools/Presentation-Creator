# Executive Summary Slide Template

An Executive Summary Slide presents the most critical insights of a presentation in a single, decision-oriented format.

It is commonly used by executives and stakeholders with limited time.

---

## Core Characteristics

- Concise
- Structured
- Action-oriented
- Outcome-focused

---

## Recommended Structure

| Section | Purpose |
|------|--------|
| Objective | Why this presentation exists |
| Context | Current situation |
| Problem | Core challenge |
| Solution | Recommended action |
| Benefits | Expected value |
| Next Steps | Decision required |

---

## Example Use Cases

- Budget approval
- Strategy alignment
- Product launch decision
- Investment evaluation

---

## Key Principle

If the audience reads only this slide, they should still understand:
- What is happening
- Why it matters
- What decision is required
