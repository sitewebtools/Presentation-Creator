# Academic Report & Research Presentation Guide

This guide outlines structured approaches for transforming academic content into clear and professional presentations.

It is designed for **students, researchers, and educators**.

---

## Academic Presentation Types

| Type | Objective | Audience |
|---|---|---|
| Research Findings | Share results | Conferences |
| Literature Review | Synthesize knowledge | Committees |
| Case Study | Apply theory | Classrooms |

---

## Standard Academic Slide Structure

| Slide | Section | Purpose |
|----|------|-------|
| 1 | Title | Identify topic |
| 2 | Abstract | Core contribution |
| 3 | Introduction | Research gap |
| 4 | Literature | Context |
| 5 | Methodology | Research design |
| 6 | Results | Evidence |
| 7 | Discussion | Interpretation |
| 8 | Limitations | Rigor |
| 9 | Conclusion | Takeaways |
| 10 | Q&A | Engagement |

---

## AI Prompt Template (Academic Context)

Act as a university professor.
Create a formal presentation outline for an academic report.
Maintain a neutral, scholarly tone and focus on clarity and logic.


---

## Presentation Tips

- Reduce text density
- Highlight findings visually
- Explain charts verbally
- Keep citations consistent
