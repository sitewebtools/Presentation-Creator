# Business Pitch Deck & Startup Presentation Guide

This document provides structured guidance and reusable AI prompt patterns for creating business pitch decks and startup presentations.

It focuses on **logic, storytelling, and decision-making clarity** rather than visual polish.

---

## Pitch Deck Categories

| Category | Primary Goal | Audience |
|------|-----------|--------|
| Investor Pitch | Secure funding | Angels, Venture Capital |
| Sales Pitch | Convert prospects | Clients, Partners |
| Internal Strategy | Align execution | Executives, Stakeholders |

---

## Core Pitch Deck Frameworks

### 1. Investor Seed Pitch

**Focus Areas**
- Problem and urgency
- Market opportunity
- Scalable solution

**Recommended Slides**
1. Vision
2. Problem
3. Solution
4. Market Size
5. Business Model
6. Traction
7. Team
8. Financials
9. Roadmap
10. Funding Ask

---

### 2. Product Launch or Sales Pitch

**Focus Areas**
- Customer pain points
- Product differentiation
- Value proposition

**Key Slides**
- Customer Problem
- Product Overview
- Key Benefits
- Competitive Comparison
- Pricing Logic

---

## AI Prompt Template (Business Context)

**Role-Based Prompt Example**

Act as an experienced startup founder preparing a pitch for senior decision-makers.
Create a structured presentation outline with one core idea per slide.
Use concise, confident language and focus on scalability, feasibility, and impact.


---

## Recommended Slide Logic Table

| Slide | Purpose |
|-----|-------|
| Title | Define vision |
| Problem | Establish urgency |
| Solution | Show differentiation |
| Market | Prove opportunity |
| Model | Explain revenue |
| Advantage | Defensibility |
| Ask | Decision required |

---

## Best Practices

- One message per slide
- Data before opinion
- Visuals support logic, not decoration
- End with a clear decision request
